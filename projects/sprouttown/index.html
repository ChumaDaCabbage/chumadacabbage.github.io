Sprout Town


Role: Lead Programmer

Project Goals: Build an engaging RTS experience within the Unity engine alongside a multidisciplinary team of artists and designers. With a focus on creating city building mechanics and visual effects.

Project Overview


Sprout Town is a 2D top-down real time strategy card game where you build a beautiful community of sprout people surviving in a harsh garden land. Wanting to mix city building mechanics with a more traditional RTS experience lead to the creation of this unique game.

This project was developed during my time at Sno-Isle Tech and was submitted to the 2022 AMES Showcase. A state wide competition between other students in Washington, where it was awarded second place.

Sprout Town is available on Itch.io! I'd love for you to give it a try and build your own Sprout Town!
